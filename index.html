I'm Alice Perucchietti, a UI/UX Designer and Architect

Hi, I'm Alice I'm a creative, dynamic person with a keen eye for detail. As a child, I dreamed of becoming a fashion designer, drawn to materials, fabrics, shapes, geometry, order, colors, and their combinations. Over time, this sensitivity naturally flowed into architecture, where I found a new way to express balance, aesthetics, and functionality. I embarked on a career in architectural design, focusing also on energy efficiency and building systems. At the same time, I’ve always been deeply interested in the well-being of people: physically, mentally, and environmentally. Over the years, I felt the need to reinvent myself, blending my technical background with a more creative, human-centered, and sustainable approach. This led me to discover my passion for digital design, particularly UX and UI, where I design intuitive, inclusive, and beautiful interfaces. I believe in design that truly speaks to people, accessible and created to make them feel comfortable. In addition to my design work, I also work as a substitute teacher, an experience that has helped me develop strong communication and teaching skills, allowing me to share my knowledge and passion with others. In my free time, I engage in practical, handmade projects, practice sports (swimming and running), travel for inspiration, and share my everyday life with my cat, my silent yet devoted desk companion.

What I do for you

User Research & Discovery

I conduct in-depth analyses to understand users' thoughts, behaviors, needs, and motivations. This process includes analyzing existing websites and defining user characteristics and actions to meet their needs through digital solutions.

01

Wireframing & Information Architecture

I design the structure of websites and applications by creating wireframes that define the layout and hierarchy of content. This approach ensures an intuitive and consistent user experience.

02

User Interface Design & Prototyping

I transform wireframes into visually appealing and functional interfaces, developing interactive prototypes that simulate user interaction with the graphical interface. This process ensures the final design is both aesthetic and usable.

03

Visual Design & Brand Identity

I create coherent and memorable visual identities for brands, focusing on elements such as logos, color palettes, typography, and social media graphics. This service aims to strengthen brand presence and effectively communicate its values.

04

Copywriting & Microcopy

I craft persuasive and engaging texts that guide users through the interface, enhancing the overall experience. This includes creating effective microcopy that facilitates navigation and interaction.

05

Digital Accessibility

I design accessible digital solutions, ensuring that websites and applications are usable by people with diverse abilities. This includes analyzing accessibility and implementing best practices to guarantee an inclusive experience.

06

User Testing & Optimization

I conduct tests with real users to identify strengths and areas for improvement in digital interfaces. I utilize specific methodologies to gather feedback and implement targeted optimizations.

07

Front-End Development (HTML & CSS)

I develop responsive and high-performing websites using HTML and CSS, translating designs into clean and maintainable code. This service ensures the final product is faithful to the design and functional across various devices.

08

Graphic Design

I unite visual aesthetics with UX principles: from icons and custom illustrations to interface-enhancing graphics, I craft assets that elevate user interfaces and reinforce brand coherence.
